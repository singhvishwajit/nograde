Why I Write?

Hi there, I am Vishwajit Singh, an independent writer.

I write to share my interpretation of the world and to sort out what I think and feel.

I share ideas in the form of essays on topics that are relevant to the human condition. If ideas on work, learning how to think, self-knowledge, personal growth, and society interest you, please check out the available essays and let me know what you think through your comments, questions, or suggestions.

I believe education is about getting people to think for themselves and therefore, the ideas and tools I share are only there to help you figure out for yourself what you think and feel is right for you.

Thank you for reading!

Vishwajit
